Klaus Info

Klaus is over 1,000 years old but has the physical apperance of a 23 year old.

Although Vampires cannot have children Klaus is only half Vampire, and the other half is Werewolf which is able to have children. His daughter's name is Hope and she uses Klaus's last name Mikaelson rather than her mother Hayley's last name Marshall.

Klaus is known as a original vampire and is feared by all the vampires. He's feared because his lack of showing mercy and his ruthlessness.
